  - juggler (you gain additional orbs)

- mines
  - butterfingers (you drop mines 1.5x as quickly)

- magic circle
  - standing in the circle heals you

- arrow
  - your arrow pierces up to <x> enemies

- red x for enemies
- blue ? for events
